Steak

Description

This steak is a recipe passed down through generations. It's super simple and tastes delicious. The type of steak doesn't matter as any steak would look beautiful with this recipe. One would say even king Arthur was drooling over such delicacy. The secret of this dish is the outstanding sauce that comes along with it. It will blow you away!

Ingredients

  • 1(2 lbs) butcher's steak
  • salt and black pepper
  • 1 tablespoon clarified butter
  • 2/3 cup chicken broth
  • 2 teaspoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons cold butter, cut into cubes
